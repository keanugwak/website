Important Disclaimer: This calculator provides estimates only and is not financial, legal or tax advice. All figures are indicative and will vary depending on your property, lender, agent, conveyancer and personal tax situation. Agent commissions are negotiable. CGT calculations are simplified — actual liability depends on your complete taxable income, deductions and ATO assessment. Land tax estimates are based on published SRO Victoria rates and do not account for aggregated holdings, trust surcharges or other adjustments — contact the State Revenue Office Victoria for an exact liability. Council and water rate adjustments depend on your settlement date and local council. Always obtain independent advice from a qualified accountant, solicitor and financial adviser before making property decisions.

Rates and thresholds reflect the 2024–25 Australian financial year. The State Revenue Office Victoria (SRO) is the authority for land tax. The Australian Taxation Office (ATO) governs capital gains tax.
